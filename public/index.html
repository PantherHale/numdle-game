How to Play

Guess a secret number between 1 and 1000.

  • Ask up to 7 questions from the MCQ picker.
  • Or fill in your own numbers in the custom blanks.
  • Then submit your best guess.
  • The AI also guesses — whoever is closer wins.
  • A new number unlocks at midnight every day.

🏆 Leaderboard Wins

A win is counted when your guess lands within 15 of the secret number (or exactly on it). This applies to both you and the AI — the leaderboard is shared.

  • Guess the secret exactly → win ✅
  • Guess within 15 (e.g. secret 400, guess 387) → win ✅
  • Guess 16 or more away → no win ❌
